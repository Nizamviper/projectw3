Beef Stroganoff

Tender strips of beef in a rich mushroom and sour cream sauce, served over noodles or rice.

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆
  • Beef sirloin strips
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion, garlic
  • Sour cream & beef broth

Preparation

Sauté onions and mushrooms. Cook beef until browned. Stir in sour cream and broth, then simmer. Serve over egg noodles or rice.
